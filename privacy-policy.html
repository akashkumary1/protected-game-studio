Privacy Policy

Protected Game Studio · Last updated: March 24, 2026

This policy applies to all apps published by Protected Game Studio.

Summary: Our apps do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All gameplay data stays on your device. We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

1. Introduction

Protected Game Studio ("we", "our", "us") develops and publishes mobile applications for Android and other platforms. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users ("you", "your"). This Privacy Policy explains how our apps handle data.

This policy applies to all apps and games published by Protected Game Studio on the Google Play Store and any other distribution platform, including but not limited to:

  • Chess Minds
  • Any future apps published under Protected Game Studio

2. Data We Do Not Collect

Our apps are designed to work fully offline (except for optional online multiplayer features). We do not collect, process, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, postal address, or any other personally identifiable information
  • Financial Information: Credit card numbers, bank accounts, payment history, or purchase records
  • Location Data: GPS location, IP-based location, or approximate location
  • Contacts: Address book, contacts list, or social connections
  • Photos and Videos: Camera access, photo library, or video content
  • Audio Files: Microphone recordings, voice data, or music files
  • Files and Documents: Files on your device, document contents, or cloud storage data
  • Calendar: Calendar events, schedules, or appointments
  • Messages: SMS, MMS, email content, or messaging app data
  • Web Browsing: Browsing history, bookmarks, or search history
  • App Activity: App usage patterns, in-app search history, or installed app lists
  • Device Identifiers: IMEI, advertising ID, Android ID, hardware serial number, or any unique device identifiers

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Our apps may store the following data locally on your device only to preserve your game progress and preferences:

  • Game save data and progress
  • User preferences (theme, sound settings, board style)
  • Game statistics (wins, losses, puzzles completed)

This data is stored using your device's local storage. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. You can delete this data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings or uninstalling the app.

4. Online Multiplayer Features

Some of our apps include optional online multiplayer features. When you use these features:

  • A temporary room code is generated to connect players
  • Chess moves and game state are transmitted between connected players in real time
  • No personal information is exchanged between players
  • No data is stored on our servers after the game session ends
  • Room data is automatically deleted within 30 minutes of game completion

Online multiplayer is entirely optional. All other features work fully offline.

5. Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate with any of the following:

  • Advertising networks or ad SDKs
  • Analytics or tracking services (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • Social media SDKs
  • Crash reporting services
  • Data brokers or data aggregation services

We do not use any third-party code that collects user data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our apps do not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Our apps are safe for users of all ages. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with any third party for any reason. Since we do not collect data, there is no data to share.

8. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted by our apps, there is minimal data security risk. Local game data stored on your device is protected by your device's own security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

9. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR, CCPA, and others), you have the right to:

  • Know what data is collected about you (none)
  • Request deletion of your data (clear app data via device settings)
  • Opt out of data sales (we do not sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

10. Permissions

Our apps may request the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET: Required only for optional online multiplayer functionality. No data is sent to external servers for tracking or analytics.

We do not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, storage, phone, calendar, or any other sensitive permissions.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Data Safety Declaration

For the Google Play Store Data Safety section, our apps declare the following:

  • No data is collected
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No data is processed
  • Data cannot be deleted because no data is collected (users can clear local app data via device settings)
